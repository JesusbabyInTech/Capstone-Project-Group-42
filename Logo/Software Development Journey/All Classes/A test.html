The Best Book I Have Read

My Top 3 Books of All Time

Act of Violence - Ryan David

This book is about a helpless woman, who was murdered in her home, while her neighbors witnessed , hoping someone would call the police because they were all too busy with their individual private lives

Shocking Pink - Erica Spindler

Three friends. Two deaths. One murderer. A tale of twisted sexual fantasy and the ripple effect on frienship

Mystic River - Dennis Lehane

A tale of three friends, with different upbringing who grew up together in a dangerous neighbourhood and how their lives turned out based on circunstances.
